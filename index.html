LogiPredict — Inventory Stockout Risk Monitor

A LightGBM classifier with monotonicity constraints that predicts stockout risk across a full product catalog. Features SHAP-powered "Why?" explanations in plain English, 7 and 14-day forward risk projections, a three-signal confidence system (BORDERLINE / VOLATILE / CAPPED), category risk aggregation, statistical reorder quantity recommendations, and one-click PDF export. Built for logistics managers who need to act in under 2 minutes. F1 = 0.790 · ROC-AUC = 0.898 on a stratified 80/20 test split.
